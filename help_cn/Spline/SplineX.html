SplineX()

语法

Result = SplineX(#Spline)
概要
Returns the spline &apos;x&apos; position in the world, after a ComputeSpline().

参数

#Spline The spline to use.

返回值

Returns the spline point &apos;x&apos; position in the world, after a ComputeSpline().

参阅

ComputeSpline(), SplineY(), SplineZ()
