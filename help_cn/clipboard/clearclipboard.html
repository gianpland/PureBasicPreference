ClearClipboard()

语法

ClearClipboard()
概要
Clears the clipboard. This means that any data contained within the clipboard, is totally flushed and is no longer available from the clipboard.

参数

无.

返回值

无.

参阅

SetClipboardText(), SetClipboardImage()

已支持操作系统

所有
