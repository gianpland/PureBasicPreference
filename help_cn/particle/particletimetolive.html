ParticleTimeToLive()

语法

ParticleTimeToLive(#ParticleEmitter, MinimumTime, MaximumTime)
概要
Change the particles time to live for the particle emitter. Every particle will live at least &apos;MinimumTime&apos; and at most the &apos;MaximumTime&apos; (in world time unit). A random value, within this range, will be used by every particle emitted.

参数

#ParticleEmitter The particle emitter to use.
MinimumTime The minimum time for a particle before being freed.
MaximumTime The maximum time for a particle before being freed.

返回值

无.

已支持操作系统

所有

<- ParticleSpeedFactor() - Particle Index - ParticleVelocity() ->
