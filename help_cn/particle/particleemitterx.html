ParticleEmitterX()

语法

Result = ParticleEmitterX(#ParticleEmitter [, Mode])
概要
Returns the particle emitter &apos;x&apos; position in the world.

参数

#ParticleEmitter The particle emitter to use.
Mode (optional) The mode to get the &apos;x&apos; position. It can be one of the following value: 
  #PB_Absolute: get the absolute &apos;x&apos; position of the particle emitter in the world (default).
  #PB_Relative: get the &apos;x&apos; position of the particle emitter relative to its parent.

返回值

The particle emitter &apos;x&apos; position in the world (according to the given &apos;Mode&apos;).
