FreeSound3D()

语法

FreeSound3D(#Sound3D)
概要
Stop and remove a 3D sound previously loaded with LoadSound3D() from memory. Once a sound has been freed, it can&apos;t be played anymore.

参数

#Sound3D The 3D sound to free. If #PB_All is specified, all the remaining 3D sounds are freed.

返回值

无.

备注

All remaining sounds are automatically freed when the program ends.

已支持操作系统

所有
