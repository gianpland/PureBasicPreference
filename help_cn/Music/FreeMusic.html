FreeMusic()

语法

FreeMusic(#Music)
概要
Stop and remove the specified music previously loaded with the LoadMusic() function from memory. Once a music has been freed, it can&apos;t be played anymore.

参数

#Music The music to free. If #PB_All is specified, all the remaining musics are freed.

返回值

无.

备注

All remaining musics are automatically freed when the program ends.

已支持操作系统

所有

<- CatchMusic() - Music Index - GetMusicPosition() ->
