GadgetID()

语法

GadgetID = GadgetID(#Gadget)
概要
Returns the unique system identifier of the #Gadget.

参数

#Gadget The gadget to use.

返回值

Returns the system identifier. This result is sometimes also known as &apos;Handle&apos;. Look at the extra chapter Handles and Numbers for more information.

已支持操作系统

所有

<- GadgetHeight() - Gadget Index - GadgetItemID() ->
