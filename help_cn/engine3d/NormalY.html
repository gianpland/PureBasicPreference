NormalY()

语法

Result.f = NormalY()
概要
Returns the &apos;y&apos; normal value at the crossed point, after RayCast(), RayCollide() or MouseRayCast().

返回值

Returns the &apos;y&apos; normal value at the crossed point.

参阅

RayCast(), RayCollide(), MouseRayCast(), NormalX(), NormalZ()

所有
